About me

Hi friend! I see you somehow stumbled upon my website and are now wondering who I am. Well, I am an undergraduate student studying towards a Bachelor of Engineering in Software Engineering. But that is not all that defines me.

I am a dreamer and one of my dreams is to be a pillar of inspiration, a leading lady. As a woman in STEM and a person of colour, I love empowering and learning more about my community and others who surround me. Helping people is always a joyful experience and I extend that joy through leadership. Its doubly rewarding in the sense that I get to learn and improve as a person while gaining satisfaction. Satisfaction, however, has many roads. I like to partake in a variety of hobbies and extraneous volunteer roles in the community to quench my ongoing thirst for stimulation. Boredom is my kryptonite that follows me everywhere I go which is why you will soon discover the lack of a pattern in my experiences.

My interest for computer science was sparked in middle school. I started off with web development and coding simple calculators on notepad with C code. From then on, my interest only grew more and more. While living the life of a perpetual student, I recently rekindled my interest for Cyber security and now yearn to learn more about AI. Even though I am studying in a field that is more prone to saturation in the consumer market, I recognize the versatility of my education and want to use that to my advantage. I want to help people. Whether directly or indirectly is up to the opportunities that the lie ahead. But what I truly desire is to work in public safety, space and defense, or support the battle for sustainability.

Now that I rambled on without saying much, have a gander at my website if you wish to learn more. Feel free to contact me with constructive criticism if something really irks you while you're browsing.

Cheerio!
- Fareen Lavji
